    Diane Rehm interview with Ray Kurzweil
by   Diane Rehm

Diane Rehm of National Public Radio's affiliate 88.5 WAMU talks with Ray Kurzweil about his book, The Age of Spiritual Machines.


Originally aired January 1999. Published on KurzweilAI.net November 7, 2001.

Diane talks with inventor, futurist and author Ray Kurzweil. In his book, Kurzweil reveals how computers and artificial intelligence will change our lives in the 21st century.
