DETERMINING PERCENTAGE OF BODY FAT FOR MEN

  • Determine your abdominal circumference at the level of your navel.
  • Measure your wrist circumference in front of the wrist bones where the wrist bends.
  • Subtract wrist circumference from abdominal circumference.
  • Determine your weight.
  • Look up your percentage of body fat in the table below.

there are reasonably cheap simple to use scales available today to give fairly accurate measurements.

TANITA makes a few that combine both regualr scale with the body fat meter by simply running a small current through the body while you stand barefoot on the scale.

OMRON make a few where a small current is passed through the body while holding eleectrodes on the instrument. (You must wigh yourself on a separate instrument).

These are all available from WEB companies or walk-in catalogue shops (such as ARGOS in the UK and Ireland). Priced $50 - $70 (US Dollars).

Much simpler than the circumference minus wrist calculations.
