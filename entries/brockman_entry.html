Brockman, John

Brockman is the president of Edge Foundation, Inc., publisher and editor of Edge, a website presenting the third culture in action, and the founder and CEO of Brockman, Inc., an international literary and software agency. A well-known computer and Internet entrepreneur and visionary, his works as author and/or editor include By the Late John Brockman, The Third Culture, Digerarti: Encounters with the Cyber Elite, editor of The Greatest Inventions in the Past Two Thousand Years, and The Next Fifty Years: Science in the First Half of the Twenty-First Century.
