Sejnowski, Terrence

Terrence J. Sejnowski, Ph.D. is an Investigator, The Salk Institute for Biological Studies. Terrence Sejnowski's goal is to discover the principles linking brain mechanisms and behavior. His lab uses both experimental and modeling techniques to study the biophysical properties of neurons and the population dynamics of large networks of neurons.
