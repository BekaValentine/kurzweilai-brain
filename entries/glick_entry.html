Glick, Jiminy

With his distinctive interview style, Jiminy Glick has become the one to watch out for among Hollywood celebs. But the road to stardom has been long and hard for Jiminy, who did time as Charles Bronson's personal assistant and a Chasen's busboy before clawing his way to the top. The climb has left him short of breath and sweating profusely. Jiminy lives in Hollywood with his wife Dixie and their sons Morgan, Mason, Matthew and Modine.
