Crustacean

Mostly aquatic mandibulate arthropods having a chitinous or calcareous and chitinous exoskeleton, a pair of often much modified appendages on each segment, two pairs of antennae and include lobsters, shrimps, crabs, wood lice, water fleas, and barnacles.
