Gateway Inc.

Gateway, Inc. has grown from a two-person start up in an Iowa farmhouse to a $9.6 billion, Fortune 250 company that employs 21,000 people around the world. Founded in 1985, Gateway went public in December 1993 (NYSE: GTW). Consistent with its vision of "humanizing the digital revolution," Gateway is about relationships and about treating customers as we would like to be treated ourselves, giving them the best value, honest advice, and great products and services.
