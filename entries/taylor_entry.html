Taylor, Robert

Former Director at ARPA who was integral to the launch of what would become the Internet. Only eleven months after the launch of the ARPAnet, Taylor created the computer science lab at Xerox Corp.'s Palo Alto Research Center (Xerox PARC). In 1983, Taylor founded Digital Equipment's Systems Research Center in Palo Alto.
