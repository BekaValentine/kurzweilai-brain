Mouse

A pointing device that is used to move a cursor on the computer screen. A mouse is moved over a flat horizontal surface, usually a rubber mouse pad, and its position is read by the computer. The original mouse had a button which the user clicks or holds down to place the cursor. Now many models have more than one button.
