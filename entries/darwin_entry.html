Darwin, Charles

Naturalist and biologist whose voyage to the Galapagos Islands on the H.M.S. Beagle led him to make observations about the evolution and diversity of species that culminated in his work "The Evolution of Species by Means of Natural Selection," which had far reaching scientific and social impacts.
