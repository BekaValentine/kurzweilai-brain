Face Recognition System

The analysis of facial features or patterns for the authentication or recognition of an individual's identity. Most face recognition systems either use eigenfaces or local feature analysis. Growing numbers of applications are starting to use face recognition as the initial step towards interpreting human actions, intention, and behavior.
