Stock, Gregory

Stock is the Director of the Program on Medicine, Technology, and Society at UCLA's School of Medicine, where he explores critical technologies poised to have large impacts on humanity's future and the shape of medical science. A prolific author and recognized authority on the impact of new technologies on human society, Stock edited Engineering The Human Germline: An Exploration of the Science and Ethics of Altering the Genes We pass to Our Children. His newest book, Redesigning Humans: How Technology Will Redefine the Human Form and Character, was published in April 2002.
