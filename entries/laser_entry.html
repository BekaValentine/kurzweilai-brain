Laser

A device that utilizes the natural oscillations of atoms or molecules between energy levels for generating electromagnetic radiation in the ultraviolet, visible, or infrared regions of the spectrum with an intensity that can burn or otherwise effect physical matter and be employed for a variety of purposes, such as precision surgery or tactical weaponry.
