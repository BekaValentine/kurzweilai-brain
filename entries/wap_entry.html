Wireless Application Protocol (WAP)

The Wireless Application Protocol is a comprehensive and scaleable protocol designed for use with mobile phones, handheld devices and wireless services such as the Short Message Service, Circuit Switched Data, Unstructured Supplementary Services Data (USSD) and General Packet Radio Service (GPRS).
