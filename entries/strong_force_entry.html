Strong Force

A fundamental physical force that acts on hadrons and is responsible for the binding together of protons and neutrons in the atomic nucleus, processes of particle creation in high-energy collisions and is the strongest known fundamental physical force. However, it acts only over distances comparable to those between nucleons in an atomic nucleus. It is also called strong interaction or strong nuclear force.
