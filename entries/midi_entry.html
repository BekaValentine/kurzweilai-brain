Musical Instrument Digital Interface (MIDI)

A way of communicating instructions for playing music from one electronic device to another. For example, from a synthesizer to a computer to a musical instrument. MIDI includes hardware (the electronic instruments and the interface between devices) and software (the set of rules for encoding and transfer of sound information).
