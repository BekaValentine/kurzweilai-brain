Nonlocality

The enforcement of correlations between separated parts of an entangled quantum systems across spacelike separations. Quantum nonlocality does not prove that "signals" travel "faster than light." Rather, it shows that at a deep level of reality the speed of light as a limiting factor is irrelevant because phenomena are instantaneously connected regardless of distance.
