Hauser, Marc D.

An evolutionary psychologist and a professor at Harvard University where he is a fellow of the Mind, Brain, and Behavior Program. He is a professor in the departments of Anthropology and Psychology, as well as the program in Neurosciences. He is the author of The Evolution of Communication, and Wild Minds: What AnimalsThink.
