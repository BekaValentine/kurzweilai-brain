Starlab

Starlab is a private, blue-sky research laboratory with campuses in Brussels and Barcelona. It is unique in the choice of its charter - the cross-fertilization between Bits, Atoms, Neurons and Genes (BANG) - as well as in its time frame (the deep future). It is the home and playground of 70 original scientists from 28 countries who believe in the wisdom of multidisciplinary research.
