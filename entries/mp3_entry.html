MP3

MP3 is a popular music download format that stands for Motion Picture Experts Group, Audio Layer 3. MP3 produces CD-quality music in a compressed file that can be transferred quickly and played on any multimedia computer with MP3 player software, at a tenth of the size of the CD format. This allows easy exchange of MP3 files over the Internet, with the aid of peer-to-peer file sharing programs like Napster.
