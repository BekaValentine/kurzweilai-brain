National Tele-Immersion Iniative

The National Tele-immersion Initiative is a project which aims to enable users at geographically distributed sites to collaborate in real time in a shared, simulated environment as if they were in the same physical room. It is the synthesis of networking and media technologies to enhance collaborative environments.
