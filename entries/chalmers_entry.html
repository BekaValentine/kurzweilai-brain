Chalmers, David

David Chalmers, Ph. D., is a Professor of Philosophy at the University of Arizona and Associate Director of the Center for Consciousness Studies. His work encompasses consciousness, artificial intelligence and computation, philosophical issues about meaning and possibility, and the foundations of cognitive science and of physics.
