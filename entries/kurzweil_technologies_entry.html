Kurzweil Technologies

Kurzweil Technologies, Inc. (KTI) is a research and development company creating and marketing technologies for pattern recognition, artificial intelligence, evolutionary algorithms, signal processing, simulation of natural processes, virtual reality, and related areas. Kurzweil "firsts" include the first omni-font optical character recognition (OCR), the first CCD flat-bed scanner, the first text-to-speech synthesizer, the first print-to-speech reading machine for the blind, the first music synthesizer capable of recreating the sounds of the grand piano and other orchestral instruments, the first commercially marketed large vocabulary speech recognition, and the first live virtual performing and recording artist.
