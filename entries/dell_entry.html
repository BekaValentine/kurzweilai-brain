Dell

Headquartered in Austin, Texas, Dell is the world's No. 1 computer systems company, and is a premier provider of products and services required for customers to build their information-technology and Internet infrastructures. The company was founded in 1984 by Michael Dell, the computer industry's longest-tenured chief executive officer, on a simple concept: that by selling computer systems directly to customers, Dell could best understand their needs, and efficiently provide the most effective computing solutions to meet those needs.
