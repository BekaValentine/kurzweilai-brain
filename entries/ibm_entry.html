International Business Machines (IBM)

IBM is a world leader in the creation, development and manufacture of the industry's most advanced information technologies, including computer systems, software, networking systems, storage devices and microelectronics. IBM was incorporated in the state of New York on June 15, 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company. Its origins can be traced back to 1890, during the height of the Industrial Revolution.
