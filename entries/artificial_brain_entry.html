Artificial Brain

The aim of this project is to build an artificial brain to control the behaviors of a life-sized robotic cat. The brain will contain around 32 million artificial neurons which live in a specially built, highly parallel computer. The project is being conducted at Starlab, a privately funded Belgian company.
