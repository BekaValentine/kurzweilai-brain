Khosla, Vinod

Vinod Khosla is a partner in the venture capital firm Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers. He was a co-founder of Daisy Systems and founding Chief Executive Officer of Sun Microsystems, where he pioneered open systems and commercial RISC processors. Khosla serves on the boards of Asera, Corio Inc., Corvis Corporation, BigVine.com, Juniper Networks, Redback and QWEST Communications, plus several other private companies.
