Advanced Network and Services

The goal of Advanced Network and Services is to advance education by accelerating the use of computer network applications and technology, successfully demonstrating and disseminating the applications and technology, catalyzing adventurous experimentation and the creation of critical infrastructure and applications, extending the evolving best practices through studies and experimentation, and participating in the creation of relevant standards and public discussions of this subject.
