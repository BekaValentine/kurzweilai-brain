Von Ehr, Jim

Jim Von Ehr is the founder, President, and Chief Executive Officer of Zyvex LLC, the first nanotechnology development company. Before starting Zyvex, he founded and built Altsys Corporation from a small programming project in his dining room to a major player in the desktop publishing industry. After he sold Altsys to Macromedia in 1995, he remained actively involved in programming, design and management.
