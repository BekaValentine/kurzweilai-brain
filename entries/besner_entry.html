Besner, Michel

Prior to co-founding Kaydara, Michel Besner worked in the technology industry, specifically in the development of bio-medical applications for cardiography. Under his guidance, Kaydara worked closely with Softimage/Microsoft for three years in the development of products. Michel has also served as a consultant in the fields of previsualization and real-time medical simulation, as well as graphics production. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Engineering from l'École Polytechnique de Montréal.
