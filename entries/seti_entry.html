Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence (SETI)

SETI was established in 1984 as an organization dedicated to the distribution of life in the universe. The SETI Institute serves as an institutional home for scientific and educational projects relevant to the nature, distribution, and prevalence of life in the universe. A primary objective of SETI is the search for extra-terrestrial life.
