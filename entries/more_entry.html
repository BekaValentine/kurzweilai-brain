More, Max

Max More is president of Extropy Institute, writer, speaker and consultant on the impact of advanced, emerging, and future technologies. He co-founded the rapidly growing Extropian transhumanist movement - a philosophical and cultural movement that brings the Enlightenment ideas of humanism into the 21st century, along with the furthering of human evolution through technological advances.
